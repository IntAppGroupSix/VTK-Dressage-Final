About Vanessa Talcott-Kendrick

Vanessa is a Brazilian-American with dual citizenship having been eduacted in Brazil and the United States. Having trained and competed early in her life with Hunters/Jumpers, she switched over fully to Dressage in the year 2000. She has successfully trained and competed through Prix St. George and has worked with many accomplished professionals in both the Hunter/Jumper world and Dressage.

In her past, she has been a working student for many Dressage greats, and has been teaching and training since 1994, for over 23 years. Teaching and training is her passion, and as she continues to grow as a horsewoman, believes when you stop wanting to learn, you stop all possibilites of progressing. Good horsemanship crosses the disciplines.
